AUCTION.COM
Android App

Auction.com is the nation's leading online real estate marketplace focused exclusively on selling residential bank-owned and foreclosed properties. We needed an Android app to catch up to the iOS app, which still needed to be launched.

The Challenge

For the Android app development, I was tasked with designing the experience. In this collaborative effort, I worked closely with the iOS designer to ensure a unified user experience across platforms.

The Method

As the mobile designer for both Web and Android platforms, I aimed to ensure that our apps were consistent and compatible with the respective operating systems. I took the OS design guidelines as a starting point to achieve this. The iOS designer and I had a working session in the middle of the project. We created a massive printout of the experiences to capture any inconsistencies.
