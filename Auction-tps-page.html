Auction.com/TEN-X
TPS Page

Auction.com is the nations leading online real estate marketplace focused exclusively on selling residential bank-owned properties and foreclosure properties. (TPS) Third-Party Sales page redesign.

Some Context

  • Third-Party Sales (TPS) is 65% of the business.

  • The product team had not established trust with the business.

  • Our TPS experience did not provide unique value to users.

  • Banks questioned the value Auction.com added to their revenue.

Our Hypothesis

Offering a better customer experience for TPS will lead to increased user engagement, resulting in higher revenue per property and improved property salability.

The Strategy

In my role as the lead designer for this project, I assisted in conducting research and collaborated with a team consisting of one other designer, four engineers, one project manager, and various business stakeholders in successfully launching the TPS PDP.

What we discovered.

  • Status change notifications
